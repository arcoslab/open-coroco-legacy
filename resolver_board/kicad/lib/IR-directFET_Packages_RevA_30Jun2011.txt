# About
The File IR-directFET_RevA_30Jun2011.mod

Contains the KiCAD module/footprint of some
directFET Transistor housings from International Rectifier.
"directFET" is a trademark of International Rectifier and the 
housings are proprietary. So be careful using them.
Please look also to International Rectifiers application note 
AN-1035 and the actual transistor datasheets.
Those footprints are untested yet!
So be nice and make a report to bernd.wiebus@gmx.de
and tell about your experiences!
