# About the File
TantalCapacitors_SMD_RevA_28Aug2012.mod


Contains the KiCAD module/footprint of some
tantalum capacitors of the size A to E (EIA 3216, 3528, 6032, 7343 and 7360).
There are different footprints for reflow, wave and handsoldering process.

TantalC_SizeA_EIA-3216_Reflow_RevA_28Aug2012

TantalC_SizeB_EIA-3528_HandSoldering_RevA_28Aug2012
TantalC_SizeB_EIA-3528_Reflow_RevA_28Aug2012
TantalC_SizeB_EIA-3528_Wave_RevA_28Aug2012
TantalC_SizeC_EIA-6032_HandSoldering_RevA_28Aug2012
TantalC_SizeC_EIA-6032_Reflow_RevA_28Aug2012
TantalC_SizeC_EIA-6032_Wave_RevA_28Aug2012
TantalC_SizeD_EIA-7343_HandSoldering_RevA_28Aug2012
TantalC_SizeD_EIA-7343_Reflow_RevA_28Aug2012
TantalC_SizeD_EIA-7343_Wave_RevA_28Aug2012
TantalC_SizeE_EIA-7360_HandSoldering_RevA_28Aug2012

TantalC_SizeE_EIA-7360_Wave_RevA_28Aug2012
